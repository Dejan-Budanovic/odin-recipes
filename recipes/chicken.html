  3. Chicken: Cut eight boneless, skinless chicken breast halves into 1-inch cubes for this sweet and sour chicken recipe.
  4. Pineapple and green pepper: Pineapple chunks and two green bell peppers (cut into 1-inch pieces) give the dish color and flavor.
